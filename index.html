Did You Know?

Oral Health Facts

  • Good oral hygiene can prevent 90% of dental diseases.
  • Your mouth harbors over 700 types of bacteria.
  • Saliva is your mouth’s natural defense against cavities.

Symptoms of Dental Problems

  • Persistent bad breath or bad taste.
  • Sensitivity to hot or cold foods.
  • Bleeding gums during brushing or flossing.

Causes of Oral Diseases

  • Poor brushing and flossing habits.
  • Excessive sugar intake and acidic foods.
  • Smoking and alcohol consumption.
